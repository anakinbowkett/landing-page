Safety & Wellbeing

Your safety is not negotiable.

Monturalearn is built for students. That means every decision we make — from how we design the platform to how we handle data — puts your safety and wellbeing first.

Need to report something?

If you ever feel unsafe, witness inappropriate behaviour, or have a safeguarding concern, contact us immediately at safety@monturalearn.com. We take every report seriously and respond promptly.

Our commitment to students

The majority of our users are under 18. We take that responsibility seriously. Monturalearn is designed to be a focused, distraction-free, and safe environment — one where students can learn without exposure to harmful content, inappropriate interactions, or pressure.

We do not run advertising. We do not sell user data. We do not allow open social features that could expose students to unknown users.

How we keep the platform safe

No advertising

Students are never targeted with ads. The platform exists solely to help them learn.

Data minimisation

We only collect what's needed to run the platform and improve learning outcomes. Nothing more.

Parental awareness

Parents and guardians can contact us at any time with questions about their child's account or data.

Safeguarding policy

We maintain a formal safeguarding policy. View it here.

Mental health & exam pressure

We know GCSEs can be stressful. Monturalearn is built to reduce that stress — not add to it. Our platform is designed around short, focused sessions that build confidence gradually rather than overwhelming students with content.

If you or someone you know is struggling with exam pressure or mental health, please reach out to a trusted adult or contact Young Minds or Childline.

Screen time & healthy study habits

We actively encourage students to take regular breaks, study in short focused sessions, and maintain a healthy balance between revision and rest. Our platform includes session prompts to remind students to step away and recharge.

Contact our safety team

For any safety concerns, safeguarding issues, or questions about how we protect young users, email safety@monturalearn.com. For general support, contact support@monturalearn.com.
