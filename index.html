Everything you need to know about Monturalearn

Monturalearn is an all-inclusive online learning platform designed to help students master their GCSEs through interactive lectures, an AI guide system, flashtile practice, live quizzes, and customisable past papers with instant analytics.

Monturalearn is the UK's first low-cost educational learning platform. Sign up for free to get started, and explore our premium features at a fraction of the cost of traditional tutoring.

Monturalearn covers all major UK exam boards including AQA, Edexcel and OCR. Our content is aligned with the national curriculum so you only ever study what's actually in your exam.

Yes! Our platform caters to both Foundation and Higher tier students. You can switch between exam tiers at any time.

Our interactive lectures break each topic down into short, focused lessons. Every lecture comes with instant questions built to mirror the real exam. An interactive AI guide walks you through each concept from zero to fully confident, with real-time progress tracking.

Flashtiles are exam-style practice cards covering every topic, difficulty level, and question type. Each flashtile reveals a full worked answer with one tap. Rate your confidence after each card — red, amber, or green — and the hard ones come back sooner automatically.

Live sessions let you compete against other students in real-time to earn Mastery Miles for each correct answer, with power-ups to shake up the leaderboard. It's a fun, competitive way to lock in what you've learned.

Yes! Monturalearn gives you access to both mock past papers with customisable questions, and real GCSE exam questions from past papers. Instant analytics show you exactly which topics you nailed and which ones are costing you marks.
