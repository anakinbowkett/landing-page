Portia, Bassanio and the Casket Plot
  • Portia is rich but cannot choose her own husband — her father's will decides.
  • Suitors must pick the right casket — gold, silver, or lead — to win her.
  • Students mix up which casket is correct — it is the lead one, not gold.
  • Q1 asks how Portia's husband is chosen — a rule from beyond the grave.
In this lecture you will learn
By the end of this lecture, you will know:
  • Who Portia is and why she cannot choose her own husband.
  • What the three caskets are and what they say on them.
  • Which suitors fail and why Bassanio succeeds.
  • How the casket plot connects to the bond plot.
