Why we exist

Private tutoring costs £40–£80 an hour. Revision guides are generic. School moves at the pace of the class, not the individual. The system was never designed for every student to thrive — it was designed for the average.

Monturalearn is built differently. We combine interactive lectures, spaced-repetition flashtiles, competitive quizzes, and real past-paper analytics into one platform — personalised to where you are and where you need to be.

What we believe

We believe every student — regardless of background, school, or starting point — has the ability to master their GCSEs. What changes outcomes isn't raw talent. It's method, consistency, and confidence.

Our job is to give every student access to the method. The rest follows.

Our promise

We don't believe in charging students more than we need to. We don't believe in locking core revision behind paywalls. And we don't believe in content that wastes your time.

Everything on Monturalearn is built to be efficient, effective, and honest. If it doesn't help you get a better grade, it doesn't belong on the platform.

Where we're going

We launched with GCSE Maths. Over time, we're expanding to cover every major GCSE subject across all UK exam boards — building the most comprehensive and affordable GCSE platform in the country.

We're just getting started.
